#speakers

Call For Papers

Join us as a speaker and make the front-end history!

We're looking for people who want to share their hands-on experience with one of the technologies related to either JavaScript or front-end development. Have you optimised your app recently so it's n times faster? That's great, come and send your papers! You know how to solve a particular problem in a different way and want to share an idea? That's fantastic! Being a speaker at meet.js Summit doesn't mean you're claimed to be the expert. It means you have worked on something cool and want to share your opinions.

All talks will be held in English. As we want to have a wide range of topics we strongly encourage you to submit your proposal. There are no trivial subjects, everyone is welcome to bring their very own view.

The length of each presentation is up to 30 minutes (including Q&A).

Being a speaker at meet.js Summit means you get a free ticket for the event.

#about-us

meet.js is the name for JavaScript meetups in Poland, organised for programmers who are interested in JavaScript, HTML and CSS. Our events have been organised for over 6 years now in 11 major business hubs in the country (Gdańsk, Warsaw, Cracow, Poznań, Wrocław, Łódź, Katowice, Szczecin, Lublin, Białystok and Bydgoszcz). They are free of charge. An average number of attendees fluctuates between 100 and 200 guests per meetup, which makes meet.js one of the largest technical community in Europe. This is a perfect place for fostering your professional network as well as recruiting new candidates.

Want to support people who share front-end passion? Let's talk! Contact us at summit2017@meetjs.pl.
